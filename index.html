What is it?

Disney World is an entertainment resort complex in Bay Lake and Lake Buena Vista, Florida, United States, near the cities of Orlando and Kissimmee. The resort comprises four theme parks (Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Disney's Hollywood Studios, and Disney's Animal Kingdom), two water parks (Disney's Blizzard Beach and Disney's Typhoon Lagoon), 31 themed resort hotels, nine non-Disney hotels, several golf courses, a camping resort, and other entertainment venues, including the outdoor shopping center Disney Springs.[1]

How to Get There

From the Airport

  • Car Rental via The Car Care Center
  • Ride With Lyft
  • Take bus 111, then transfer to the bus 50

From Seattle (step by step)

  1. Take 1-Line from Pioneer Square Station to SeatTac/Airport Station
  2. From Seattle(SEA) to Orlando(MCO) by flight
  3. Take bus #111 from Oia Transfer Center to Sea Harbor Drive And Grand Vacations Way. Then, transfer to the bus #50 from Central Florida Parkway And Sea Harbor Drive to Wdw Transportation & Ticket Ctr
  4. Walk to Walt Disney World from Wdw Transportation & Ticket Ctr
